    • ESU Steering Committee

    This webpage will present the various teams behind this 2025 edition of the European Summer School in Digital Humanities. Besançon and the university Marie and Louis Pasteur have been awarded the organization of the ESU 2025 by the Steering Committee of the ESU created in 2024. A “local team” is dealing with all the organizational aspects of the ESU in Besançon. A scientific committee, combining members of the “local team” as well as workshop instructors will be dealing with the evaluation of all received applications.
